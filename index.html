About Us

Flora’s Flower Shop is a fourth generation family business with locations in Watertown, Carthage and Clayton, NY. We offer daily delivery to all of Jefferson County and Fort Drum, as well as parts of Lewis County. Flora’s shops are open seven days a week with the exception of Christmas Day.

We offer complete floral and gift service for any occasion, including green and flowering plants, arrangements for every taste, a large selection of cut flowers... Flora’s service extends to complete wedding floral service, sympathy floral consultation and corporate gift giving programs. Weekly cash and carry specials are a local favorite, and all of our products come with a 100% Customer Satisfaction Guarantee!
